🧭How we teach this (your learning profile)

Your strengths — we lean on these

  • You restate ideas in your own words — our #1 tool. Every checkpoint, you explain it back.
  • You ask surgical questions and demand to know where formulas come from — so we always derive, never assert.
  • You connect to the real world and form your own analogies (the "linked list").

What overwhelms you — we avoid these

  • Many new symbols at once → one example, carried through. Never two at once.
  • Abstract before concrete → real numbers first, names second.
  • Word collisions (data/weight, error/loss) → every term defined once, up front.
  • Unexplained numbers / long prose → state every number's origin; use tables, visuals, runnable code.
The loop every module follows:   concrete number → one tiny step → you restate it → I confirm → checkpoint → next
